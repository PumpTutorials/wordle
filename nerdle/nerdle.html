Nerdle

For people who love numbers and calculations. Nerdle is a game inspired by mathematical equations. You will have to guess 8-character equations with numbers and operations of addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division. Just guess the characters that appear in the calculation and calculate the correct result, Will this make it difficult for you after 6 guesses? Numbers and calculations are shown in the correct place in green; yellow indicates these characters are present in the equation but are in the wrong place, gray indicates a character that does not appear in the equation. Show your love of math with Nerdle, helping you think faster and more accurately. There is only one equation for you each day.
